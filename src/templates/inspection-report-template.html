BEDROOM 1 - CROSS-CONTAMINATED ITEMS
✓ SALVAGEABLE (Professional Treatment Required)
• Clothing items stored in wardrobe
• Personal belongings on shelves
• Non-porous items (hard surfaces, electronics casings)
• Books and documents (if structurally sound)
• Hard plastic containers and organizers
• Metal and glass decorative items
Treatment Protocol: HEPA vacuuming followed by antimicrobial solution application as per MRC standards.
✗ NON-SALVAGEABLE (Disposal Required)
• Porous soft furnishings with direct mould contact
• Items with visible mould penetration
• Damaged or deteriorated personal items
• Items with compromised structural integrity
• Tenant-elected items not to be salvaged
Disposal Protocol: Double-bagged and properly disposed of according to contamination protocols.
