## COMMON SCENARIOS & CAUSES

### Condensation Issues:
- Primary cause: "Elevated humidity levels without active moisture control"
- Recommend: Dehumidifiers (run at 55-60% RH), improved ventilation, exhaust fans
- Language: "condensation due to elevated humidity", "inadequate ventilation"

### Water Leaks (Resolved):
- State clearly: "Previous [type] leak has since been resolved/repaired"
- Add: "No further recommendations required regarding the leak source"
- Focus on: Remediation of residual contamination

### Water Leaks (Active/Suspected):
- State: "Suspected [source] leak" or "confirmed [source] leak"
- Recommend: "Engage [specialist] to assess and repair prior to MRC attending"
- Emphasize: Must fix source before remediation

### Ventilation Problems:
- Inadequate exhaust fans: "Replace with higher capacity unit (80+ CFM)"
- Incorrect positioning: "Relocate exhaust fan directly above shower"
- No exhaust fan: "Install ceiling exhaust fan"
- Add: "Fit with run-on timer (15-20 minutes post-shower)"

### Subfloor Issues:
- Ground water: "Engage drainage specialist"
- Poor ventilation: "Install mechanical subfloor ventilation system"
- Combined issues: Address both drainage AND ventilation
